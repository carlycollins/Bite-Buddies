## Brief Project Introduction
Bite Buddies is a meal-planning and social app designed to
help busy college students save time, reduce food costs, and
eat healthier. The app creates personalized meal plans based
on users’ preferences, budget, schedule, and pantry items,
and can generate grocery lists automatically.
It also includes a social feature that allows students to
connect with friends, collaborate on meals, and receive group
restaurant recommendations. By combining smart planning
with social interaction, Bite Buddies makes eating well more
convenient and engaging for students.

## Summary of User Research
We conducted interviews with fellow college students to understand their struggles with
cooking and food planning.
Common challenges included lack of time, financial stress, repetitive meals, and reliance
on convenience foods. Many students wanted to eat healthier and cook more but felt
overwhelmed by planning and budgeting. These insights confirmed the need for a
simple, flexible tool that supports affordable and realistic meal planning for college
lifestyles.
